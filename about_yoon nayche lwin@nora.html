ABOUT ME



Hello! My name is Yoon Nayche Lwin or Nora, a 14-year-old Burmese teenager who loves to travel even if it is occasionally. My family often brought me on trips when I was a kid so over time, I gained more interest in travelling. Now I really enjoy going on trips especially with my family on holidays. I mostly go on local trips to different parts of my country, diving deep into our culture and history.

I decided to start this blog in order to keep track of all my trips and also to share my journeys and let people become inspired by my tales to travel.






My greatest pleasure is to travel to various places with my family, trying out new foods, doing new activities both indoors and outdoors, going sightseeing, capturing the moments and having fun wherever we go.









I'm from Yangon, Myanmar and this is my location
